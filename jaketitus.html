About Jake

Jake Titus is a dedicated Sales Associate at Robert Jones Roofing who specializes in helping Florida homeowners navigate the often-stressful process of getting a new roof. Jake is known for his deep product knowledge and his ability to make even complex roofing decisions feel simple and manageable.

From the initial free inspection through to the final walkthrough, Jake is with you every step of the way. He's particularly skilled at helping customers choose between Atlas shingle roofing and Premier Metal systems - and explaining the long-term value of each option for Florida's demanding climate.

Contact Jake for your free estimate and experience the Robert Jones Roofing difference firsthand.
