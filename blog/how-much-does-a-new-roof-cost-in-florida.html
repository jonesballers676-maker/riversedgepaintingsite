How Much Does a New Roof Cost in Florida?

Published Nov 12, 2025 • 2 min read

← Back to Blog

If you’re a Florida homeowner wondering how much it costs to replace your roof, you’re not alone. A roof replacement is one of the most important investments you can make to protect your home, and it’s natural to want a clear idea of what to expect.

At Robert Jones Roofing , we believe in transparency from the start. While roof costs can vary depending on materials, size, and labor, this guide breaks down what Florida homeowners typically pay in 2025 and what factors can affect your total cost.

💰 Average Roof Replacement Cost in Florida

On average, most Florida homeowners spend between $10,000 and $20,000 for a full roof replacement. Smaller roofs with basic asphalt shingles may cost closer to $8,000 , while larger homes or premium materials like metal and tile can reach $30,000 or more .

Here’s a quick breakdown by material type:

• Asphalt Shingle Roofs: $4.50 – $7.50 per square foot

• Metal Roofs: $8.00 – $12.00 per square foot

• Tile Roofs: $12.00 – $18.00 per square foot

Homes in coastal areas like Titusville, Cocoa Beach, and Merritt Island may experience slightly higher costs due to stricter wind codes and the need for specialized installation.

⚙️ Factors That Influence Roof Costs

Every home is different, but here are the most common factors that impact roof pricing in Florida:

• Roof Size & Pitch: Larger or steeper roofs require more materials and labor.

• Material Choice: Asphalt is the most affordable option, while tile and metal offer greater durability and lifespan.

• Decking & Underlayment Condition: Any wood rot or water damage underneath your shingles may require replacement.

• Permits & Labor Rates: Local regulations and professional installation standards can affect the total cost.

• Insurance Claims or Storm Damage: In some cases, part of your replacement may be covered by homeowners insurance.

🧾 Getting an Accurate Estimate

Online price ranges are helpful, but the only way to know your exact cost is through a professional inspection. At Robert Jones Roofing , we offer free inspections and detailed written estimates , so you’ll always know what to expect before any work begins.

Our team measures your roof, checks for hidden damage, and explains your options, helping you make the best decision for your home and budget.

🌴 How to Save Money on a New Roof

Replacing a roof doesn’t have to break the bank. Here are a few ways Florida homeowners can save:

• Explore financing options — flexible payment plans make large projects manageable.

• Check your insurance coverage — storm or wind damage may qualify for reimbursement.

• Look into local or state programs — some Florida homeowners can qualify for roof assistance programs.
