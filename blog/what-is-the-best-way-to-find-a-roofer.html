What is the Best Way to Find a Roofer?

Published Apr 2, 2025 • 3 min read

← Back to Blog

Finding a reliable roofer can be a daunting task, especially when your home’s protection is on the line. Whether you're dealing with storm damage, a leak, or you're ready for a complete roof replacement, hiring the right professional ensures peace of mind and long-term durability.

If you're searching for Brevard County roofers , this guide will walk you through the best steps to choose a skilled and trustworthy roofing contractor — and introduce you to a reputable local expert you can count on.

1. Ask for Referrals and Read Online Reviews

Start by asking friends, neighbors, or coworkers if they’ve had a positive experience with a roofer. Word-of-mouth recommendations are often the most trustworthy. Then, turn to platforms like Google Reviews, Yelp, and Angi to research potential roofers’ reputations.

For example, Robert Jones Roofing in Brevard County is known for its exceptional craftsmanship and outstanding customer service. With consistently high reviews and strong community ties, it’s a name that locals trust.

2. Verify Licensing, Insurance & Experience

Make sure the roofer you hire is fully licensed and insured. A qualified roofer should also provide a written estimate and warranty for both materials and labor. Experience in your local area is another plus, as it ensures familiarity with regional weather patterns and permitting processes.

When reviewing Brevard County roofers , confirm how long they've been operating and ask about their portfolio. Robert Jones Roofing , for instance, is well-established in the area and specializes in both residential and commercial projects.

3. Request Multiple Quotes & Compare Thoroughly

Don’t settle for the first estimate. Get at least three quotes and compare them not only on price but also on the scope of work, materials used, timeline, and warranty coverage. Watch out for unusually low bids — they might indicate shortcuts or subpar materials.
