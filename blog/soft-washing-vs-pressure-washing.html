Soft Washing vs Pressure Washing: What Florida Homeowners Need to Know

Published Apr 10, 2026 • 2 min read

← Back to Blog

If you’re a homeowner in Titusville or the surrounding Space Coast, you’ve probably noticed dark streaks or green buildup on your roof or siding. That’s not just dirt—it’s algae, mold, and mildew thriving in Florida’s humid climate.

The big question is: should you pressure wash it or soft wash it?

Choosing the wrong method can actually damage your roof and cost you thousands. Here’s everything you need to know.

What Is Soft Washing?

Soft washing is a low-pressure cleaning method that uses specialized solutions to safely remove algae, mold, mildew, and bacteria from exterior surfaces.

Unlike pressure washing, it doesn’t rely on force—it relies on proper cleaning agents that kill growth at the root .

Soft washing is ideal for:

• Roofs (shingle & tile)

• Siding

• Stucco

• Fences

• Screens & lanais

Why Pressure Washing Can Damage Your Roof

Pressure washing uses high-powered water that can:

• Strip away protective granules from shingles

• Void your roof warranty

• Force water under shingles causing leaks

• Crack tiles or damage siding

For asphalt shingle roofs (which most homes in Florida have), pressure washing is not recommended .

Why Soft Washing Is the Best Choice in Florida

Florida’s climate creates the perfect environment for Gloeocapsa Magma (the algae that causes black streaks).

Soft washing:

• Kills algae at the source

• Prevents rapid regrowth

• Extends the life of your roof

• Improves curb appeal instantly

• Is recommended by roofing manufacturers

How Often Should You Soft Wash Your Roof?

Most homes in Titusville should be soft washed every:

👉 1–3 years , depending on:

• Tree coverage

• Shade

• Moisture exposure

• Proximity to water (river/lagoon areas grow algae faster)

Signs You Need a Soft Wash ASAP

• Black streaks on your roof

• Green moss or algae

• Dirty or faded siding

• Stained driveway or walkways

If you see any of these, it’s time to act before it causes permanent damage.

Why Homeowners Trust Professional Soft Washing

DIY cleaning might seem cheaper, but using the wrong mix or pressure can lead to:

• Roof damage

• Plant damage

• Uneven cleaning

• Quick regrowth

A professional soft wash ensures:

✔ Proper chemical ratios

✔ Safe application

✔ Even, long-lasting results

If your roof or home exterior is showing signs of algae or buildup, now is the time to take care of it before it turns into costly damage.
