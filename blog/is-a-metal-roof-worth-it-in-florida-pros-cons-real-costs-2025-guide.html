Is a Metal Roof Worth It in Florida? Pros, Cons & Real Costs (2025 Guide)

Published Nov 19, 2025 • 2 min read

← Back to Blog

When homeowners in Florida start thinking about replacing their roof, one of the first questions they ask is: “Is a metal roof worth it?” Between the intense heat, hurricane-strength winds, and heavy rain, choosing the right roofing material matters more here than almost anywhere else.

At Robert Jones Roofing, we install both shingle and metal systems, and we always encourage homeowners to start with a free roof inspection so you can get a clear understanding of your home’s condition before deciding.

Benefits of a Metal Roof in Florida

✔ Extreme Wind Resistance

Metal roofs can withstand 140–180 mph winds depending on the system. During your free roofing estimate , we can help you compare wind ratings and see what fits your home best.

✔ Long Lifespan

Metal roofs commonly last 40–70 years. If you want to know how long your current roof will last, you can schedule a free roof inspection with Robert Jones Roofing and get a full report.

✔ Superior Heat Reflection

Metal roofing reflects heat and can reduce energy bills. If you're trying to decide whether it's worth the investment, click here to request your free roofing quote and compare options.

✔ Fire & Impact Resistant

Great for hail and debris during storms. If storm damage is a concern, we recommend starting with a no-cost inspection from our roofing team .

Potential Downsides to Consider

✖ Higher Upfront Cost

Metal costs more upfront, but our team can walk you through actual pricing during a free metal roof estimate .

✖ Noise Without Proper Underlayment

With proper installation and underlayment, noise isn’t an issue. If you want to review underlayment options, you can contact Robert Jones Roofing anytime.

✖ Not Every Home Needs Metal

Sometimes a high-quality shingle roof is the better choice. We’re always happy to look at your home and recommend the best system during a [free roof evaluation] .

Metal Roof Cost Breakdown in Florida

• Shingle roof replacement: $8,000 – $20,000

• Metal roof replacement: $18,000 – $45,000

For an exact number tailored to your home, schedule a free roofing estimate in Titusville with our team.
