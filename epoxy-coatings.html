Why prep matters in Florida

Humidity pushes moisture through concrete slabs. We test for vapor, diamond-grind profiles to spec, and install primers designed for your slab condition. Skipping that sequence is why cheap epoxy fails—and we don’t cut corners.

Systems we install

  • 100% solid color epoxy bases with polyaspartic or urethane topcoats
  • Flake broadcasts in custom blends to match vehicles or brand colors
  • Cove bases and drainage-friendly details for wash-down areas
  • Anti-slip aggregates calibrated for barefoot patios vs. warehouse boots
